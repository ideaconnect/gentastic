Getting started

  1. Download & unzip the portable ZIP anywhere - no admin rights needed.
  2. Run Gentastic.App.exe. On first launch it detects your GPU and recommends a backend (CUDA, Vulkan, or CPU) - confirm or override it.
  3. Open the Models page and download a model. FLUX.2 klein 4B is the default - small, fast, and needs no license token.
  4. Go to Generate, type a prompt, and hit Generate. That's the whole workflow.
